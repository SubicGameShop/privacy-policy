Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Subic Game Shop ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you play Enchanted Garden (the "Game") on Android.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information. If you sign in, we collect your email address and a unique user ID through Firebase Authentication (Google). Guest play is also supported without providing any personal information.
  • Game progress. Your level progress, star ratings, gem balance, and in-game purchase history are stored in Firebase Firestore so your progress is saved across devices.
  • Device information. Standard device identifiers (device model, OS version, advertising ID) are collected automatically by our third-party services for analytics and advertising purposes.
  • Purchase information. In-app purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment details. We receive a confirmation of purchase from Google Play to credit your in-game gem balance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To save and sync your game progress across devices
  • To deliver daily login rewards and track streak progress
  • To process in-app purchases and credit your gem balance
  • To show relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To improve game performance and fix bugs

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The Game uses Google AdMob to show rewarded video ads. AdMob may collect and use your device's advertising ID and other data to serve personalised advertisements. You can opt out of personalised ads at any time in your device's Settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

For more information on how Google uses data from apps that use its services, please visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

4. Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and cloud saves: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google AdMob — advertising: support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543
  • Google Play Billing — in-app purchases: play.google.com/about/play-terms

5. Data Retention

Your game progress and account data are retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email address below.

6. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Game after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
